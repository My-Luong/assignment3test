Volant

Description:

Volant is a race that has existed since ancient times, predating Terestrium by thousands of years and having pure-blooded elf heritage. However, the elves did not focus on economic or social development; instead, they established a harmonious society by primarily relying on plants and natural energy.

Politics:

Conflict among Volant members is rare as they trust in the balance and harmony of nature.The people of Volant concentrate on maintaining balance and protecting nature, believing that the survival and development of the world depend on the harmony between humans and the environment. One of their crucial tasks is to safeguard Vitalia Spring, a creature that generates pure energy-infused minerals. Volant ensures that this resource does not fall into the hands of other species that could disrupt the balance and harm the world.

Geography: Volant tends to inhabit mild climates, such as the Pentagon mountain range, Virmit, and Lancer.

Ability:

While being a peaceful race, Volant is not always in a state of tranquility. As an ancient species facing threats from other races, Volant has developed special combat abilities to protect themselves and maintain balance in a harsh world. They possess heightened senses, exceptional speed in both running and flying, and the ability to manipulate elements such as water and mist. Additionally, their proficiency with archery and swordsmanship is commendable.Volant is regarded as a race that values peace and strives to maintain the harmony of the world.

Culture:

Each member of Volant contributes to the collective mission and vision of their race. They are often seen as guardians and protectors of natural balance. Their important role involves sustaining a healthy environment and safeguarding vital resources. The presence of Volant serves as a reminder of the necessity for balance and respect for nature. Despite their thousands of years of development, Volant maintains a simple way of life and a deep connection to nature. Their creativity and adaptability in surviving and thriving in challenging environments have made them a powerful and esteemed race on Aristo.
