Egalit

Description:

Egalit In fact, this is another branch of Terrestrium, but their earliest ancestors had contact with the Volant, an ancient race, and were taught and instructed by them in some basic magic. Egalit from then on began to familiarize themselves with magic and, with their inherent curiosity, they often live as wandering nomads across the lands. However, Egalit also has some small settlements in the form of villages at the foot of the "world tree", a colossal and sacred tree, where they live a peaceful and cozy life. Children usually leave the village to live elsewhere, except on special occasions. Each village in the Egalit settlements has a system of civil organization and they manage their daily activities autonomously.

Politics:

They enjoy living harmoniously with other races and seek cooperation and cultural exchange. They willingly undertake missions from other nations and become reliable magical advisors and adept explorers.

Ability:

Egalit inherited magical abilities from the Volant, but over time, they have developed and evolved into unique and distinctive abilities. For example, they have the ability to use invisibility magic to become invisible for a short period or create powerful spells to manipulate the environment. They also possess illusionary abilities, allowing them to create visual phenomena to attract attention or deceive opponents.These special magical abilities make Egalit formidable in battles and explorations. They can skillfully and flexibly utilize magic, taking advantage of the environment and resources around them to gain strategic advantages.

Culture:

Their living environment is often surrounded by lush vegetation and abundant nature. Egalit tends to live in harmony with and respect nature.They believe that protecting the world tree, their living habitat, is an obligation and a way to repay the Volant who passed down magic to them. With a peaceful and fair-minded nature, Egalit often supports peace and justice in political matters.
