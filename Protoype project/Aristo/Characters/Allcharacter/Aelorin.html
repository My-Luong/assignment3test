King Aelorin

A solemn Elf ruler who carries the weighty responsibility of maintaining balance and harmony in the world. With his regal presence and deep connection to nature, he is seen as a wise and revered figure by his subjects. King Aelorin embraces his role as a guardian of the natural order, ensuring that all living beings coexist in harmony.
