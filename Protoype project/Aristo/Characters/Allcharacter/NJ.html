N.J

A powerful and elusive witch hailing from the enigmatic Egalit race. Known for her mastery of dark magic and her shadowy influence, she operates in the shadows, leaving a trail of chaos and destruction in her wake. Few have seen her face or know much about her, but her reputation as a terrorist and wanted criminal has spread far and wide.
