Kaldur Blackthorn

A renowned blacksmith hailing from the fearsome Humus race. He is widely regarded as the most skilled and formidable blacksmith in the world, renowned for his craftsmanship and ability to forge weapons of unparalleled power. What sets Kaldur apart from other blacksmiths is his mastery of black magic, which grants him the unique ability to replicate any weapon he lays his eyes upon, regardless of its origin or cultural heritage.
