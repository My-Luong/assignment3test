Orion Shadowblade

A skilled assassin hailing from the enigmatic Egalit race. However, his path to becoming an assassin took an unexpected turn as he was adopted by the village chef at a young age. Despite his unconventional upbringing, Orion holds an unwavering loyalty and devotion to his adoptive father, willing to go to any lengths to protect him and repay the kindness shown to him.
