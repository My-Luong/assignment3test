Seraphina Nightshade

A skilled and lethal assassin hailing from the secretive Egalit race. Known for their unparalleled stealth and precision, the Egalit are a race of assassins who operate in the shadows, carrying out covert missions with deadly efficiency. Seraphina's life has been shaped by tragedy, as her brother was kidnapped by the Terrestrium race, leading her to embark on a personal quest for vengeance and to reunite with her missing sibling.
