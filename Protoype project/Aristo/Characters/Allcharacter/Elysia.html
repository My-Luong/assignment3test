Elysia Evergreen

An enigmatic elf who possesses a complex identity, being aware that she is a half-breed, with her father belonging to the bloodthirsty Humus race. This knowledge has set her apart from her elven heritage and created a sense of estrangement from her own kind. Despite these challenges, Elysia embraces her unique lineage and strives to find her place in the world while seeking understanding and acceptance.
